About us

Nelly Skinner

Speech and Language Therapist (Paediatrics)

Bsc Hons Speech and Language Sciences, Newcastle University

Sue Skinner

Occupational Therapist (Paediatrics)

Professional and Operational Lead in Children's Occupational Therapy

Sue and Nelly have over 35 years of experience between them working in children and young people's services in the NHS. They have delivered a range of therapy interventions and worked with children of pre-school age, children in mainstream schools, and children with complex additional needs such as Autistic Spectrum Disorder. Sue has particular expertise in children with sensory processing disorders and Nelly has particular experience in working with pre-school aged children with language delay, and phonological disorders.
A unique service provision using the Care Aims a child centred evidence approach. Giving the child and young person and those around them every day the tools to meet their goals and aspirations.
