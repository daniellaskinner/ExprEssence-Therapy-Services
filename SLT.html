Speech and Language Therapy

Speech and Language Therapy Occupational Therapy

We treat a range of Speech, Language, and Communication Needs (SLCN) including:

  • Language Delay
  • Developmental Language Disorder
  • Phonological Delays and Disorders (speech disorders)
  • Articulation Difficulties
  • Childhood Apraxia of Speech
  • Stammering
  • Children who need Alternative Augmentative Communication (AAC)
  • Selective Mutism
  • Social Communication Difficulties

The first point of contact with our Speech and Language Therapy Service would involve between one to three sessions of initial assessment. This will involve discussion with you (parents) as well as formal assessment with your child; and may involve liaison with staff in your child's setting (early years staff, teachers, teaching assistants, child-minders etc.) The service can the provide blocks of tailored 1:1 therapy and professional reports can be written, as desired.
